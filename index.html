About the Scholarship

Our mission: to make equestrian learning accessible for youth—across riding, driving, clinics, and hands-on horsemanship—especially for students who might not otherwise have access.

Driftless Riders Scholarships is organized exclusively for charitable and educational purposes. We raise and distribute scholarship funds to support young people in Northeast Iowa (and the wider Driftless area) in learning, developing, and sustaining equestrian skills—both in the arena and beyond.

We believe barns should be welcoming, inclusive spaces. Scholarships help cover lesson fees, clinics/workshops, and structured programs that build confidence, responsibility, and lifelong horsemanship.

How to Apply

Scholarships are available to youth who show strong interest in horse education and clear financial need. Eligible programs include structured riding and driving lessons, clinics with certified trainers/clinicians, and recognized equine/youth organizations that provide formal instruction.

The application includes:

  • A short questionnaire (personal info, experience, goals)
  • A 300–500 word personal statement describing your interest, how a scholarship helps, and any barriers to access
