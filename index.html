About the Scholarship

The Driftless Riders Scholarship is a public benefit corporation organized exclusively for charitable and educational purposes under Iowa Code Sections 504.141.34 and 504.1705.3. Our mission is to raise and distribute funds for scholarships to help youth in the Northeast Iowa region learn, improve, and maintain equestrian (horse-riding and related) skills—especially those who would not otherwise have access to these opportunities.

How to Apply

Scholarships are available for youth riders in Northeast Iowa who demonstrate interest and financial need. The application process includes a short questionnaire and a personal statement.

Applications due November 15
Board reviews & scholarship recipients notified December 1.
