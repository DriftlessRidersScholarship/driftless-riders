About the Scholarship

Our mission: to make equestrian learning accessible for youth‚Äîacross riding, driving, clinics, and hands-on horsemanship‚Äîespecially for students who might not otherwise have access.

Driftless Riders Scholarships is organized exclusively for charitable and educational purposes. We raise and distribute scholarship funds to support young people in Northeast Iowa (and the wider Driftless area) in learning, developing, and sustaining equestrian skills‚Äîboth in the arena and beyond.

Scholarships help cover lesson fees, clinics/workshops, driving instruction, and structured programs that build confidence, responsibility, and lifelong horsemanship.

How to Apply

Scholarships are open to youth who demonstrate both a genuine interest in equestrian education and a clear financial need. Eligible activities include structured riding and driving lessons, clinics with certified trainers/clinicians, horsemanship programs, and recognized youth equine organizations offering formal instruction.

The application includes:

  • A short questionnaire (personal info, experience, goals)
  • A 300‚Äì500 word personal statement describing your interest, how a scholarship helps, and any barriers to access

Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. If you need help with the application or accommodations, please contact the board.
